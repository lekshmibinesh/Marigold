Marigold

French Marigolds (Tagetes patula): French marigolds are small, bushy plants with flowers up to 2 inches across. Their blooms are described as single, anemone, or crested according to their appearance and occur in yellow, orange, or mahogany-red shades. Single types have one outer row of petals.
